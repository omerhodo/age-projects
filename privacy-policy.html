We use Google AdMob to show advertisements in our app. AdMob may use advertising identifiers and may collect information such as:

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is minimal risk to your privacy. All age calculations are performed locally on your device.
